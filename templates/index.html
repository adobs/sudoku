About Sudoku

Each puzzle consists of a 9x9 grid containing given clues in various places. The objective is to fill all empty squares so that the numbers 1 to 9 appear exactly once in each row, column and 3x3 box.


To play, click on one of the numbers (1-9) below the 9x9 grid. Then click the cell in the board you would like the number to go.


If you need a little help, click the 'Hint' button!
